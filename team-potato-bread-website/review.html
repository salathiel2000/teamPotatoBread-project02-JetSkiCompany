This was my first time ever in The Bahamas and also my first time riding a jet ski. Salathiel and Thomas were absolutely brilliant, they were able to make me feel extra comfortable with the Seadoo Spark, and even gave me an extra 10 mins. of training (without charging) to ensure I was truly ready to have the most fun on my ride.
